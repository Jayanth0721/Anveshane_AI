About Anveshane AI

A modern tender intelligence workspace for admins, bidders, and citizens

Tender Intelligence

Infrastructure for transparent procurement decisions

Anveshane AI turns tender files, bidder proposals, amount comparisons, and audit signals into a reviewable decision layer that procurement teams can trust and citizens can understand.

Document Understanding

PDF and DOCX tenders are converted into searchable text, then scanned for eligibility rules such as GST, PAN, experience, certifications, turnover, amount, and delivery period.

Evidence-Based Evaluation

Each bidder is evaluated against extracted criteria with reasons and source signals, so admins can review why a submission passed, failed, or needs manual attention.

Smart Ranking

The system considers compliance, quoted amount, and promised completion speed to help surface the strongest proposal, while still leaving final publishing control with the admin.

Controlled Transparency

Bidders do not see internal scores during review. Citizens only see awarded outcomes after the admin closes applications, reviews evaluations, and publishes the result.

Explainability Timeline

Each tender now includes a lifecycle timeline showing creation, submissions, AI evaluations, admin reviews, and publication milestones in one ordered view.

Contractor Audit

Admins can access a real-time points table for contractors, tracking merit points, demerit points, and success rates based on project history and on-time completion.

How the Evaluation Flow Works

1

Admin creates a tender with deadline, value, duration, and tender document.

2

Contractors submit proposal documents before the application closes.

3

AI extracts eligibility signals, proposal amount, and delivery timeline.

4

Admin reviews the evaluation ledger and closes the application.

5

Admin publishes the result, making the awarded tender visible to bidders and citizens.

Total Evaluation Scale
ST + SOB + SP = 10.0
⚠️ Warning: Sum should ideally equal 10.0 for standard normalization.

LLM Evaluation Methodology

The AI calculates a total score out of 10.0 using the following strict caps and mathematical normalization:

  • Tender Match (ST): Max 4.0. Alignment with technical & commercial requirements.
  • Order Book (SOB): Max 3.0. Pipeline health, financial capacity & backlog.
  • Performance (SP): Max 3.0. Historical execution, timely delivery & quality.
Total Score = ST + SOB + SP
